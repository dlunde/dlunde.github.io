About me

I am a first year PhD student at the Software and Computer Systems department of KTH Royal Institute of Technology. My main research interests are:
  • Programming language theory and compilers
  • Probabilistic modeling and statistical inference
  • Statistical machine learning
My principal supervisor is David Broman (KTH), and my assistant supervisors are Lawrence Murray (Uppsala University) and Christian Schulte (KTH).
